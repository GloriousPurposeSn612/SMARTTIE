# 🧠 SMARTTIE  
## Sequential Medical Analysis and Real-world Treatment Trajectory Intelligence Engine

---

## 📌 Project Description

SMARTTIE is an AI-powered medical intelligence system designed to transform scattered online patient discussions into structured, meaningful treatment insights.

When patients are diagnosed with a condition, they often turn to the internet to understand real experiences beyond clinical descriptions. They search for answers like:

- What side effects did others face?
- How long did recovery actually take?
- Did symptoms worsen before improving?
- What were the long-term outcomes?

However, this information is highly fragmented — spread across forums, blogs, social media threads, and community platforms. While valuable, it exists as unstructured text, making it difficult to analyze systematically or derive reliable patterns.

SMARTTIE addresses this challenge by using Generative AI and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to:

- Aggregate publicly available treatment discussions
- Extract symptoms, side effects, medications, and outcomes
- Identify recovery timelines and sequential treatment stages
- Detect patterns across multiple patient journeys
- Convert unstructured narratives into structured treatment intelligence

The system builds a **Sequential Treatment Trajectory Model**, allowing users to understand how real-world treatment journeys typically unfold over time.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The primary goal of SMARTTIE is to bridge the gap between clinical knowledge and real-world patient experiences by creating interpretable, data-driven treatment trajectories.

It aims to:
- Reduce information overload
- Provide structured recovery expectations
- Enhance patient awareness
- Support informed medical conversations

---

## ⚠ Disclaimer

SMARTTIE is an informational intelligence tool and does not replace professional medical advice. All treatment decisions should be made in consultation with qualified healthcare professionals.

---

## 🌍 Vision

Healthcare transparency should not depend on scrolling through endless anecdotal threads.

SMARTTIE envisions a future where real-world treatment intelligence is structured, accessible, and actionable — empowering patients with clarity instead of confusion.
